Who is CDAD?

As Detroit's association of Community Development and Neighborhood Improvement Organizations, Community Development Advocates of Detroit (CDAD) is a catalyst for the trasformation of our neighborhoods, led and inspired by residents, community-based organizations, and ohter community stakeholders.

CDAD works to enhance the capacity and effectiveness of Detroit's commmunity-based organizations, initiatives, and residents through advocacy, training, technical assistance, networking opportunities, information sharing, and facilitating common action.
